Hello! I'm a Ph.D. student in Computer Engineering at Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Pau Closas. I specialize in Bayesian machine learning, federated learning, and GNSS security. I received dual Bachelor's degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC) through the CFIS program, a selective interdisciplinary center.

My research develops principled and robust machine learning models, currently focusing on jammer localization in crowdsourced GNSS environments. I have a strong background in probability theory, statistical modeling, optimization, and deep learning.
